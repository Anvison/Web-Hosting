About Us

Harvard Business School (HBS) is a prestigious institution located in Boston, Massachusetts, renowned for its commitment to excellence in business education. Established in 1908, HBS has been at the forefront of educating leaders who make a difference in the world. Our mission is to create and disseminate knowledge that helps organizations and individuals achieve their full potential. Programs MBA Program The HBS MBA program is a two-year, full-time program that provides a transformative experience through a rigorous curriculum, real-world case studies, and a diverse community of peers. Students develop critical leadership skills and a deep understanding of business fundamentals. Executive Education HBS offers a range of executive education programs designed for senior leaders and executives. These programs provide the skills necessary to navigate the complexities of modern business and drive organizational change. Doctoral Programs Our doctoral programs prepare the next generation of scholars and researchers. HBS offers several Ph.D. tracks, including Business Economics, Organizational Behavior, and Strategy, among others.
